Walks in the Park

This page was originally created by Colleen van Lent as part of the Walks in the Park project, an effort to inspire people to spend time outdoors, enjoy the natural beauty of parks, and discover the calming effect of walking in green spaces. The project is meant to be open and collaborative, inviting participants to add their own experiences, reflections, and creative ideas to enrich the content. Whether it’s sharing stories about a peaceful morning stroll, documenting the changing seasons, or highlighting the plants and wildlife that make each park special, every contribution helps this page grow into a collective journal of outdoor exploration. Just as every walk in the park can reveal something different—a new trail, a hidden corner, or a moment of quiet reflection—this page is designed to evolve with the input of its community, creating a shared resource that celebrates nature, wellness, and connection.
