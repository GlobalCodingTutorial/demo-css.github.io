Neighborhood Walks

This page was originally created by Colleen van Lent as part of the Neighborhood Walks project, an initiative designed to encourage people to explore their local communities and notice the small details that make each neighborhood unique. The project is meant to be collaborative and evolving, so participants are warmly invited to contribute their own observations, photos, stories, and suggestions to enrich the experience for everyone. By making changes, adding new perspectives, or improving the design of this page, you are helping to build a shared space that reflects the diversity and character of different neighborhoods. Just like a walk through familiar streets can reveal something new each time, this page is intended to grow and adapt with the ideas of its contributors, creating a living resource that celebrates community, connection, and discovery.
